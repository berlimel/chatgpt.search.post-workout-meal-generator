Post-Workout Meal Suggestions

Based on your recent workout, here's a meal suggestion to aid in muscle recovery:

  • Grilled Salmon with Quinoa and Broccoli: Grill salmon fillets and serve with a side of cooked quinoa and steamed broccoli. This meal provides high-quality protein and complex carbohydrates, essential for muscle repair and replenishment.

Remember to stay hydrated and adjust portion sizes based on your workout intensity and personal energy needs.
